Running costs

Know exactly what your tenant owes — and what you absorb

South African landlords deal with COJ accounts, body corporate levies, water, electricity, and refuse — split between what tenants pay and what you cover. PropMaster makes this clear every month.

  • ✓ Toggle each cost as Tenant pays or Owner pays
  • ✓ Recoverable costs auto-populate the next invoice
  • ✓ Monthly summaries show total cost vs total recovery
  • ✓ Upload COJ or levy PDF — AI extracts every line item
